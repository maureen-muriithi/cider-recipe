Cider Cocktail Recipe

Don't we love a sweet cold cocktail! This is a secret recipe to making a cider cocktail.

Ingredients

  • 60ml (Double shoy) southern comfort
  • 180ml (about 3/4 cup) Tusker Cider
  • Apple Juice or (Ginger Ale)

Garnish

  • Apple Slices
  • Sticks of cinammon
  • Sprig of thyme

Directions:-

  1. Before beginning, ensure that the Tusker Cider and the Apple Juice are well chilled so that you dont have to add ice cubes.
  2. In a short glass add in the Southern Comfort followed by the chilled Tusker Cider.
  3. Top up the glass with Apple Juice (or Ginger Ale if using).
  4. Garnish with slices of quartered apple, a stick of cinnamon and a sprig of fresh thyme.
  5. Serve immediately
